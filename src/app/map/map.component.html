U-Learn DATA VISUALIZATION

  • In this portal, you can easily find an organization working in a settlement within a specific capacity. To start, click on the name of a settlement to see which organizations are working there. You can select more than one settlement. To unselect, click on the name again. Click on the next button to proceed. You will be taken to an advanced page search where you will see the organizations that are working on the selected settlements.
  • In the advanced search page, you can select multiple filters at a time to narrow down the search. You can select from either the drop-down menu on top or from the circular filters on left, or with a combination of both. As you select a filter, it will be shown to your left. You can click on the x button beside the filter to clear that filter. To remove all filters click on the "clear all" option on top or the "reset" option on the left. If you already know the name of an organization, you can search for it on the search bar.
  • As you search or use filter, you will see cards with organization names reflecting your search. Click on any of the cards to learn more about the organization and their work. You can also view the filtered data as a table by clicking on the "view Filtered Data as table" option. Furthermore, you can export that data as CSV or PDF.
